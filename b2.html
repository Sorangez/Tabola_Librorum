Within the pages of Billions Under Pressure, Jarrett Preston, world-class asset trader and CEO of Idoneus, offers masterful wisdom for creating, exchanging and preserving value. He has spent decades helping the world's Elite trade some of the most valuable possessions on earth--yachts, castles, islands, artwork, gemstones--and now his knowledge in the field of asset trading and value preservation is in your hands. The world of value is ever-changing, and Preston is your guide--he will walk you through:

  • The history of value exchange, dating back thousands of years
  • Modern methods of growing and protecting tangible asset portfolios
  • Application of blockchain technology to secure and trade your valuable holdings
  • Tested-and-true methods of assessing value, both on a transactional and personal level
  • Strategy to mitigate risk and increase odds of continued wealth into the future
  • The hidden value of time and strategic relationships
  • Identifying assets that are personally fulfilling and valuable to you
    • And so much more! If you're stuck in a multimillion-dollar asset with seemingly no way out, pick up this book and find the key to your golden handcuffs. Security and freedom are at your fingertips with Billions Under Pressure.
